Audio Samples from mandarin TTS model based on Google's Tacotron

Author: KJW
(Update at May 31, 2019)

Introduction

This speech synthesis model is developed from Google's Tacotron model (Here's the paper) and trained by Biaobei open source database (contains 10h female clear recordings). After 100k-steps training, this model has been able to synthesize relatively influent and vivid speech and able to deal with most context conditions. However, due to the limited data volume and insufficient training(I think), there still are unnatural rhythm and perceptible current sounds in synthesized audios. Since this subject is still on going, I believe significant improvement will be made soon. Following are selected samples:

Practical application

(Synthesize dub for an introduction video.)
"测区为待测区域，发射源位置放置发射机，测线与测点位置按照设计书规定进行布置，放置接收机。 面积测量时，如测区范围内发现有意义的异常时，应及时加密测线。 如因测深曲线异常或失去连续性，也应加密测线。 另外，测点尽可能不选在山顶或者深沟处。应选择相对开阔，地势平坦的地方布置发射机。 布置结束后，操作员需检查各通道信号，并根据信号大小选择前置放大器的增益，确定无误后，方可进行测量和记录。 测量完毕后，对所得数据进行处理及反演解释，得到有用信息。"

Nevertheless

When trying to synthesize a sentences with special terms or phrases that unlikely to appeared in training database, there will be some troubles. It is hard for a 10-hours-old young model to read a text that she don't know what it is talking about.
